When connected and streaming. Which layout direction feels right?

Navigation: Sidebar vs Top Bar

A

Left Sidebar

Vertical nav with icons + labels. Compacts well, room to grow (settings, devices, stats). Familiar pattern from Spotify, Discord, VS Code.

B

Top Tab Bar

Horizontal tabs across the top. Simpler, fewer elements. More room for the content area. Less scalable with many views.
